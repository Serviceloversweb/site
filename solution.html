Our solution

The customers can rate the service experience either via
the in-store tablet or with their own phones by scanning a QR code

Try out the mobile rating flow by scanning this QR code with the camera on your smartphone

Servicelovers social app

Employees installs the Servicelovers mobile app on thier phones and can now recieve personal ratings comming from the customers, build their service CV, and manage their Servicelovers profile.

Dashboard

You get full access to the Servicelovers web dashboard where all the ratings can be viewed. From the dashboard you can:

  • See what customers think about the service level in your business.
  • See what stores performs the best.
  • See what customers might find lacking in your business.
  • Manage your tablets eg. setup premade feedback answers eg. “The que was too long” or “The product was not in stock.” etc.
